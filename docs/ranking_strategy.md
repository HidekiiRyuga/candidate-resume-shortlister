# Ranking Strategy

## Philosophy

The JD explicitly states that keyword matching alone is a trap.

Therefore the ranking engine will prioritize:

1. Semantic relevance
2. Relevant production experience
3. Retrieval / ranking background
4. Evaluation knowledge
5. Behavioral signals

---

## Positive Signals

- Product company experience
- Ranking systems
- Recommendation systems
- Retrieval systems
- Production deployments
- Evaluation metrics
- Python

---

## Negative Signals

- Research-only career
- Consulting-only career
- Marketing / non-AI roles
- Keyword stuffing
- Long inactivity

---

## Final Score

Semantic Match      → 40%

Experience          → 25%

Skills              → 20%

Achievements        → 10%

Behavior            → 5%
